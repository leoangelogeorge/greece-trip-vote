Naxos
Option A: "Base Camp Naxos" — Most Budget-Friendly

The plan: All 6 nights on Naxos.

Why: #1 rated family island in Greece. Sandy beaches stay ankle-deep for 50m out (great for kids). 40% cheaper than Santorini. Excellent vegetarian tavernas.

Splitting up: Day-trip to Paros (40 min ferry) or Santorini (1.5h ferry).

Estimated cost: €900-2,500 per couple for 6 nights

✓ Cheapest, most relaxed, best for kids, no extra ferry logistics

✗ Less "wow factor" than Santorini, only one island

The plan: 3 nights Paros → split: 3 nights Santorini OR stay on Paros/hop to Naxos.

Why: Everyone gets 3 days together on Paros. Then Santorini fans take a 1.5h ferry, others stay on Paros or hop to Naxos (40 min).

Option C: "Hidden Gems" Naxos + Milos — Most Unique

The plan: 3 nights Naxos → 3 nights Milos.

Why: Naxos for family-friendly beaches. Milos for otherworldly landscapes, sea caves (Kleftiko boat tour!), turquoise water — as photogenic as Santorini but less crowded and cheaper.

Splitting up: From Milos, 1.5h ferry to Santorini for anyone who wants to visit.

Estimated cost: €1,000-2,500 per couple for 6 nights

✓ Unique experience, great value, less crowded, Instagram-worthy

✗ Two ferry travel days, Milos is less "classic Greece postcard"

Logistics to Know

  • August is peak season — book accommodation in the next 2-3 weeks.
  • Meltemi winds can delay high-speed ferries (conventional ferries are more reliable).
  • Ferry booking: Ferryhopper.com
  • Villa/apartment rentals can be much better value than hotels for our group.
  • Inter-island ferries: Paros↔Naxos (40min), Paros↔Santorini (1.5h), Naxos↔Milos (2-3h).
