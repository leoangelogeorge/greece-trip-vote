The Setup

  • After Ola & Awse's wedding in Athens (Aug 6-8), we have 6 nights (Aug 9-15) to explore the Greek islands together.
  • The group: 4 couples + 2 kids (including a 3-year-old).
  • Starting point: Athens (Piraeus port).
  • Everyone is vegetarian-friendly.
  • We're open to splitting up for parts of the trip if people want different things.

Quick Island Cheat Sheet

Island Vibe Best For Daily Cost (couple) Ferry from Athens
Naxos Relaxed, traditional, sandy beaches Families & budget €150-400 3.5h, ~€43/pp
Paros Trendy, charming villages, great food Couples & foodies €200-450 3h, ~€41/pp
Milos Dramatic geology, sea caves, turquoise Adventurers €150-400 2.5h, ~€42/pp
Santorini Iconic caldera, sunsets, luxury Bucket-listers €300-600 5h, ~€46/pp

Daily costs include accommodation + food + activities. Ferries between neighboring islands: €15-30/person, 30min-2h.

A note on Santorini with toddlers: Steep stairs, unprotected drops in caldera villages, no stroller access, volcanic pebble beaches (not sandy). Doable as a day trip/overnight, but probably shouldn't be our main base.
Option A: "Base Camp Naxos" — Most Budget-Friendly

The plan: All 6 nights on Naxos.

Why: #1 rated family island in Greece. Sandy beaches stay ankle-deep for 50m out (great for kids). 40% cheaper than Santorini. Excellent vegetarian tavernas — fresh produce, local cheese, traditional dishes.

Splitting up: Day-trip to Paros (40 min ferry) or Santorini (1.5h ferry) for those who want to explore.

Estimated cost: €900-2,500 per couple for 6 nights

✓ Cheapest, most relaxed, best for kids, no extra ferry logistics

✗ Less "wow factor" than Santorini, only one island

Option B: "Split Base" Paros + Santorini Recommended

The plan: 3 nights Paros → then split: 3 nights Santorini OR stay on Paros/hop to Naxos.

Why: Everyone gets 3 days together on Paros (great food, beautiful villages). Then Santorini fans take a 1.5h ferry, while others stay on Paros or hop to Naxos (40 min) for a cheaper, more relaxed second half.

Splitting up: The group naturally splits by preference and budget. Paros, Naxos, and Santorini are all within 40min-1.5h of each other.

Estimated cost: €1,500-3,000 per couple for 6 nights

✓ Gives everyone options, respects different budgets, Paros is the sweet-spot island

✗ Requires coordinating a split, slightly more logistics

Option C: "Hidden Gems" Naxos + Milos — Most Unique

The plan: 3 nights Naxos → 3 nights Milos.

Why: Naxos anchors the family-friendly beach days. Milos offers otherworldly landscapes, sea caves (boat tour to Kleftiko is a must!), turquoise water — as photogenic as Santorini but far less crowded and cheaper.

Splitting up: From Milos, it's 1.5h by ferry to Santorini for anyone who still wants to visit.

Estimated cost: €1,000-2,500 per couple for 6 nights

✓ Unique experience, great value, less crowded, Instagram-worthy

✗ Two ferry travel days, Milos is less "classic Greece postcard"

Logistics to Know

  • August is peak season — we should book accommodation in the next 2-3 weeks.
  • Meltemi winds in August can occasionally delay high-speed ferries (conventional ferries are more reliable).
  • Ferry booking: Ferryhopper.com is the easiest way to book.
  • For our group size, villa/apartment rentals can be much better value than hotels.
  • Inter-island ferries: Paros↔Naxos (40min), Paros↔Santorini (1.5h), Naxos↔Milos (2-3h).
